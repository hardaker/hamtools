sig2kml Example Output

This map was generated using the sig2kml application. The data was collected by having my FT-857D monitor a simplex channel as I rode around my house on various paths while transmitting from a handheld at 1 Watt (clipped to my waste). The GPS kept track of my location and signalrecord kept track of transmission levels.
